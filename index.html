Bombay Bites offers something different to the traditional Indian takeaway. We've taken the great British love affair for Indian food and created a simple, quick and affordable way to enjoy it. Our menu allows you to order the classic favourites but most of our customers go for one of our delicious layered Bombay boxes. A self-contained meal conveniently eaten straight from the box.
